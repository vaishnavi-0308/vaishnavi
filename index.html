CHICKEN

Butter chicken is a dish made by marinating a chicken overnight in a yoghurt and spice mixture. The chicken is then roasted or baked. A sauce is made from butter, tomatoes, almonds and various spices, usually including the famous tandoori masala spice mix, and sometimes cream.It is the restaurant's most loved!.

BEEF

The restaurant's special beef dish is a rare fillet of Aberdeen Angus beef with sectiona special mixing of spicy tomato sauce and equal proportion of Madeira sauce , splattered with fine green beans and good amount of cheese and butter . One of the Restaurant's finest!.

SUSHI

The restaurant's "Sushi Mexicano" is a mouth-watering dish comprising of special Sushi rolls accompanied by variety of spices along with the Chiles Toreados(Habanerro and Seranno) fried , diced and tossed along with the dish!.
